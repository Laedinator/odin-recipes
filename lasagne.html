Lasagne

Description

This recipe will show you how to make this delicious traditional italian dish.

Ingredients

  • Large bowl, safe to put in the oven
  • Lasagne pasta sheets
  • ~1l Bolognese sauce
  • ~0.5dl Béchamel sauce
  • Grated cheese
  • Some butter

Preperation

  1. Warm the oven according to information on the package of the pasta sheets.
  2. Use the butter to grease the bowl (prevents sticking).
  3. Put some Béchamel sauce in the bowl (Be aware that you need more to cover off the last layer of Pasta, see step 9).
  4. Add a layer of pasta sheets. You can break off some edges to make sure you can cover as much as possible of the bowl.
  5. Add some Bolognese sauce, the pasta should be covered.
  6. Add some Béchamel sauce (does not need to cover the Bolognese sauce).
  7. Add another layer of pasta sheets.
  8. Repeat the steps to create those layers until the bowl has around 0.5cm left (prevents dripping over the edge of the bowl).
  9. Use the rest of the Béchamel sauce to cover the last layer of pasta.
  10. Cover the bowl with aluminium foil (keeps moisture in).
  11. Bake the Lasagne according to the instructions on the pasta.
  12. Take the foil away in the last 10 minutes and put the grated cheese on.
  13. When its done, take the Lasagne out, put the foil on again and let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
  14. Enjoy!
