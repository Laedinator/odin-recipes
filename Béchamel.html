Sauce Béchamel

Description

This Sauce is widely used in a variety of dishes. It also serves as a great base and you can add easily change or add more spices for a different taste.

Ingredients

  • 50g butter
  • Flour
  • 0.5l Milk
  • Nutmeg
  • Pepper and salt

Preperation

  1. Melt the butter in a pan on medium-high heat.
  2. Add some flour (2-3 dinner spoons full).
  3. Stir until the flour starts to be well mixed with butter. Reduce heat a bit
  4. Add all the milk
  5. Add the spices to taste
  6. Stir until the Sauce is on the boiling point and take heat off.
  7. Keep stiring until the Sauce is thick and doesnt stick to the pan anymore
  8. It is now ready to serve or use for another dish
